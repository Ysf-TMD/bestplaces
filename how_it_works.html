How it works

Assess your HR practices and validate your employee experience

We’re the employee experience specialists, who deliver accurate data, powerful insights, and facilitate positive change within your organization

4 weeks certification journey

Project Kickoff (1 week)

You will receive all the supporting documentation as well as the communication toolkits to prepare the launch of the program

HR Assessment and Employee Survey (2 weeks)

Your HR Team will be invited to complete the HR assessment and your employees will be invited as well to score your organization around the 8 workplace factors in a completely secure and confidential platform

Analysis and validation (1 week)

The analysis and validation of the collected information will take you beyond statistics to create an accurate picture of your engagement index and how your HR practices are aligned with our people framework standard

Certification

If your company scores beyond the threshold index, you made it !! Your company is certified as a Best Place To Work and you can now enjoy the benefits of the certification

Transform the culture of your organization

The ultimate goal is to trigger a transformation of your organization’s culture. The important thing is to help organizations understand the results and benchmark them so that they can reflect on ways in which they can improve engagement and employees trust over the long term.

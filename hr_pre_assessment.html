HR Pre-assessment

Take our free HR Pre-assessment

You can take advantage of our complimentary HR Pre-assessment to determine your eligibility for certification. If you believe you may benefit from a preparatory strategy prior to initiating the certification process, we are here to assist.

By participating in our HR pre-assessment, you will complete an online questionnaire designed to evaluate the practices implemented within your organization pertaining to the various areas examined during the certification procedure. Achieving an average score of 70% or higher confirms your readiness to obtain the certification.

Take our free HR Pre-assessment

“Best Places to Work” is the most definitive ‘Employer of Choice’ certification that organizations aspire to achieve. The program certifies and recognizes leading workplaces in many countries around the world through a proprietary assessment process. This HR Pre-Assessment provides companies with insights into their ability to achieve the certification. Our HR consultant will discuss with you the results and the next steps to enroll in the certification process.

HOW IT WORKS

1

Assess what you are doing as an employer to support your employees

Complete the pre-assessment by answering series of questions about your company's HR practices around topics like: leadership, people management, wellbeing, performance management and giving back.


Sample questions:
  • Do you have initiatives focusing on employee wellbeing ?
  • Do you have an onboarding program?
2

Get your results

We evaluate your responses and based on the alignment with the certification requirements, you will receive a score to confirm your readiness to achieve the certification.

A summary report can be provided with access to free tools to help you prepare for the certification journey, including customized improvement reports, best practice guides, and case studies.

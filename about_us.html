Our History

The Best Places to Work program is the flagship employer of choice program driven in partnership with Best Companies Group. BCG is a well established US company with the purpose of identifying and recognizing best employers in many countries around the world and places of employment that are leading the way in defining the employee experience of the 21st century.

The program started its international expansion in 2010 with leading programs covering more than 60 countries certifying world’s best-known organizations in partnership with many local and international established media companies.

Our accreditation is one of the most respected and comprehensive measures of workplace culture and talent management best practices.

HOW IT WORKS

1

Invite companies to get the Best Place to Work certification

2

We’ll work with them to see if they are eligible for certification.

3

You’ll get a gift card when they finish the certification process.

who you should refer

Best Places to Work primarily works with HR and leadership teams. If your referral is a company founder, a senior leader, or the head of people they could be the perfect fit. Feel free to refer companies in any country.

TERMS & Conditions

  • Gift card values are in USD.
  • Participants may redeem a gift card to a business of their choice from our select vendors.
  • Best Places to Work will pay out the reward to a referring party within 30 days of the referral’s certification.
  • No reward will be provided for companies in an active certification cycle.
  • No reward will be provided if a company has already been referred to Best Places to Work team for the certification. Only one reward will be granted per referral company.
  • Best Places to Work reserves the right to declare a referral ineligible for the promotion if we believe the referral program is being abused.
